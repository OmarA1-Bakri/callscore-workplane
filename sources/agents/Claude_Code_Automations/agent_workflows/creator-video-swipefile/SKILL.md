# Creator Video Swipefile

Builds reusable video-marketing references for the configured app.

## Workflow

1. Load `marketing.app.yaml`, strategy, channel plan, and research folder.
2. Identify relevant creators/videos for the app audience.
3. Capture hook, structure, proof mechanism, CTA, thumbnail pattern, comments/audience signal, and why it works.
4. Translate the pattern into safe assets for active channels only.
5. Record gaps where dedicated profiles, prompts, or automations should be created.

## Safety

Do not copy creators verbatim. Extract patterns and adapt them to the configured app's compliance rules.
