# Marketing Community Drops

Creates rules-aware community drafts for app launches and research drops.

## Workflow

1. Load `marketing.app.yaml` and referenced strategy/channel/compliance docs.
2. Confirm requested community channel is active.
3. Research or record the community rule context before drafting.
4. Draft short, transparent, non-spam copy.
5. Include disclosure/caveats required by the app compliance doc.
6. Attach `SEND_GATE` and community-rule checklist.

## Output contract

- community/channel;
- rule assumptions or checked rules;
- draft post/comment/message;
- why it is relevant to that community;
- caveats included;
- approval/gate required.

## Safety

Never send DMs, submit community posts, or bypass community rules without exact approval.
