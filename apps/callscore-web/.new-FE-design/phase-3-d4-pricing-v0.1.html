Questions before you pay us.

Is the plan.pro.trial trial actually free?
Yes — no card required. You get everything in plan.pro.name for plan.pro.trial. At the end you pick a plan or fall back to Watch. No auto-charge.
What if I cancel mid-month?
You keep plan.pro.name through the end of the billing period, then drop to Watch. No prorated refunds, no surprises — cancel from settings.
Do you give financial advice?
No. We score creators on their published directional calls and show you the evidence. You decide what to do with it. Nothing on CTR is a recommendation.
How is the score calculated?
Log-return α on resolved calls · tier-weighted by creator · windowed to last 90d with a freshness decay. Full methodology in Score Explanation — nothing hidden.
Can I pay in crypto?
Not at launch. Card + Apple/Google Pay only. Stablecoin payments on the Team plan are on the roadmap.
What's in Team that's not in Pro?
Shared watchlists/saved views, SSO + audit log, CSV/API export, auto-generated weekly memo + retraction digest. Designed for 3-10 seat research desks.
